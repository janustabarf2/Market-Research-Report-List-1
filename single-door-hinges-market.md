<p><p>The single door hinges market includes various types designed for specific applications. Barrel hinges are cylindrical and allow for a smooth pivot, while butt hinges are commonly used for residential doors, providing support at the frame. Pivot hinges enable doors to rotate from a single point, while HL hinges combine two features for stability. Concealed hinges are hidden when the door is closed, offering a clean look, and flag hinges allow for quick door removal. Other types cater to specialized needs.</p></p>
