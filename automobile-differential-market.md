<p><strong>The Automobile Differential Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anti Slip Differential</li><li>Double Worm Differential</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile differential market includes various types designed to enhance vehicle performance. Anti-slip differentials prevent wheel spin by distributing power to the wheel with the most traction, improving stability and handling. Double worm differentials use a complex gear system for better torque distribution, offering smoother power delivery. Other market types encompass limited-slip differentials, which balance power between wheels, and open differentials, which allow for independent wheel rotation. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing vehicle dynamics under various driving conditions.</p></p>
