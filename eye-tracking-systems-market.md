<p><p>Eye tracking systems are categorized into two main types: remote eye tracking and mobile eye tracking. Remote eye tracking systems utilize fixed cameras to monitor eye movements from a distance, often employed in research, usability studies, and user experience evaluation. In contrast, mobile eye tracking systems are wearable devices that track eye movements in real-world environments, allowing for dynamic data collection during activities such as shopping or driving. Both types provide valuable insights into visual attention and user interaction behaviors.</p></p>
