<p><strong>The Automotive Thermostat Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Insert Thermostat</li><li>Housing Thermostat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive thermostat market comprises two primary types: insert thermostats and housing thermostats. Insert thermostats are standalone components designed to regulate coolant flow within the engine by opening and closing based on temperature variations. In contrast, housing thermostats are integrated into a single unit, providing housing for the thermostat while facilitating fluid movement. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining optimal engine temperature, enhancing performance, and improving fuel efficiency, which are critical for modern vehicles.</p></p>
