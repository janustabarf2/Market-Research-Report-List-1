<p><p>The PEM electrolysis catalysts market is primarily categorized into two types: Oxygen Evolution Reaction (OER) catalysts and Hydrogen Evolution Reaction (HER) catalysts. OER catalysts facilitate the generation of oxygen during water splitting, while HER catalysts optimize the production of hydrogen. Both play crucial roles in improving the efficiency and performance of proton exchange membrane (PEM) electrolysis systems. The development of advanced materials for these catalysts is vital for increasing the energy output and reducing costs in renewable energy applications.</p></p>
