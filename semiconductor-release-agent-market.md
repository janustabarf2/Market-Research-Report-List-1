<p><p>The semiconductor release agent market consists of two primary types: resin-based and rubber-based agents. Resin-type release agents are formulated from various resins that provide excellent separation properties and surface finish, making them suitable for high-precision applications. Meanwhile, rubber-type release agents, often made from silicone or fluoropolymer materials, are known for their flexibility and ease of application, offering effective release in molding processes. Both types are essential in optimizing manufacturing efficiency and product quality in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
