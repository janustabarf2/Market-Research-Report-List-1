<p><ul><li>DEXA Technology</li><li>NIR Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Meat Fat Analysis Equipment market includes technologies like DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) and NIR (Near-Infrared Spectroscopy). DEXA technology utilizes X-ray beams to assess fat distribution and composition in meat, providing accurate measurements for quality control. NIR technology, on the other hand, analyzes the unique absorption patterns of infrared light to determine fat content and other components in meat without destruction, offering rapid results. Both methods enhance meat quality assessment and support industry standards.</p></p>
