<p><p>The Magneto-Rheological Damper market is primarily categorized into two types: active dampers and semi-active dampers. Active dampers utilize an external power source to control the damping force, allowing for real-time adjustments based on road conditions and vehicle dynamics. In contrast, semi-active dampers do not require power for operation; instead, they enhance performance by adjusting the viscosity of the magneto-rheological fluid in response to changing conditions, providing improved comfort and stability without a fully powered system.</p></p>
