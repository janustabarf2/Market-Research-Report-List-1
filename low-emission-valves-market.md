<p><strong>The Low Emission Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trunnion-mounted Valves</li><li>Floating Valves</li><li>Rising Stem Ball Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Emission Valves market comprises various types designed to minimize emissions during operation. Trunnion-mounted valves feature a fixed shaft that provides stability and reduced leakage, suitable for high-pressure applications. Floating valves utilize gravity to seal against the pipeline, allowing for flexibility and reduced wear. Rising stem ball valves feature a stem that rises as the valve opens, providing clear visual indication and effective sealing, making them ideal for precise control in critical applications. These specialized valves play a key role in environmentally conscious industries.</p></p>
