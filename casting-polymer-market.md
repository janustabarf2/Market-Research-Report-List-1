<p><p>The casting polymer market includes three primary types: Solid Surface, Engineered Stone, and Cultured Marble. Solid Surface materials are versatile, non-porous, and customizable, often used for countertops and sinks. Engineered Stone combines natural stone with resin for durability and aesthetic appeal, making it popular for high-end surfaces. Cultured Marble blends resin and minerals to create visually appealing surfaces that mimic natural marble, often used in bathroom fixtures. Each type offers unique benefits, catering to diverse consumer preferences and applications.</p></p>
