<p><ul><li>Fully Polarized Light</li><li>Partially Polarized Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Polarization Cameras market is segmented into two primary types based on light polarization: fully polarized light cameras and partially polarized light cameras. Fully polarized light cameras are designed to capture images using light waves that oscillate in a single direction, yielding high contrast and clarity, making them ideal for demanding industrial applications. Partially polarized light cameras, on the other hand, utilize light waves that oscillate in multiple directions, offering versatility and improved performance in varied lighting conditions, often suited for more general applications.</p></p>
