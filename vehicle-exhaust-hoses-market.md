<p><p>The vehicle exhaust hoses market comprises three types based on construction layers: single-layer, double-layer, and three-layer hoses. Single-layer hoses are lightweight and flexible, suited for simpler exhaust systems. Double-layer hoses offer enhanced durability and heat resistance, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Three-layer hoses provide the best protection, combining insulation, strength, and resistance to harsh conditions, suitable for heavy-duty vehicles. Each type serves specific needs in terms of flexibility, heat tolerance, and environmental resistance, impacting their overall performance.</p></p>
