<p><p>The low voltage contactors market is primarily segmented into AC contactors and DC contactors. AC contactors are designed for alternating current applications, commonly used in industrial motor control, lighting, and HVAC systems. They facilitate the safe control of large loads. In contrast, DC contactors handle direct current applications, often utilized in battery management systems, electric vehicles, and renewable energy systems. Both types ensure efficient switching and control, catering to varying energy requirements across different sectors.</p></p>
