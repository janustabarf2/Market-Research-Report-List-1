<p><p>The vacuum salt market encompasses two primary types: granular vacuum salt and fine vacuum salt. Granular vacuum salt, characterized by its larger particle size, is commonly used in industrial applications, such as chemical processing and de-icing. In contrast, fine vacuum salt features smaller, uniform particles, making it suitable for food processing, culinary uses, and pharmaceuticals. Both types are produced through the evaporation of brine under a vacuum, ensuring high purity levels and minimal impurities for various applications in different sectors.</p></p>
