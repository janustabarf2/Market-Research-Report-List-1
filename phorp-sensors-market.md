<p><p>The pH/ORP sensors market includes two primary types: glass type sensors and ISFET sensors. Glass type sensors measure pH levels through a glass electrode that interacts with hydrogen ions, providing accurate readings in various applications. ISFET sensors, or Ion-Sensitive Field-Effect Transistor sensors, utilize semiconductor technology to detect pH levels, offering advantages like miniaturization and resistance to harsh conditions. Both sensor types play crucial roles in environmental monitoring, industrial processes, and water quality testing, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
