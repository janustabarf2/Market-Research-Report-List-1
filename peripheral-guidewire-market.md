<p><strong>The Peripheral Guidewire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Guide Wire</li><li>Angled Guide Wire</li><li>J-Shape Guide Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The peripheral guidewire market comprises three primary types: straight guidewires, angled guidewires, and J-shaped guidewires. Straight guidewires offer a straightforward insertion path, ideal for reaching distal vessels. Angled guidewires feature a pre-made angle at the tip, enhancing navigation around obstructions or tortuous anatomy. J-shaped guidewires have a curved design, facilitating access in challenging vascular environments, such as bifurcations. Each type is tailored for specific procedural needs, enhancing the precision and efficacy of peripheral vascular interventions.</p></p>
