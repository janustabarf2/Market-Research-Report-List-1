<p><ul><li>Sequential Type</li><li>Simultaneous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ICP spectrometer market is divided into two main types: sequential and simultaneous. Sequential ICP spectrometers analyze elements one at a time, providing high sensitivity and resolution, making them suitable for applications requiring accurate trace element analysis. In contrast, simultaneous ICP spectrometers measure multiple elements simultaneously, offering faster results and higher throughput, ideal for large-scale analyses. Each type serves distinct analytical needs, influencing their adoption across industries such as environmental monitoring, materials science, and food safety.</p></p>
