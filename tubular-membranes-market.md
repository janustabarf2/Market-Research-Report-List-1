<p><p>The Tubular Membranes market comprises technologies such as Membrane Bioreactors (MBRs) and Reverse Osmosis (RO). MBRs integrate biological treatment with membrane filtration, effectively removing contaminants and producing high-quality effluent for wastewater treatment. Conversely, RO utilizes pressure to force water through a semi-permeable membrane, effectively desalting and purifying water by removing salts and impurities. Both methods are essential for efficient water management and are widely used in municipal and industrial applications for sustainable resource conservation.</p></p>
