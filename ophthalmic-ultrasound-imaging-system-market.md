<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ophthalmic Ultrasound Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A- Scan</li><li>B- Scan</li><li>Combined Scan</li><li>Pachymeter</li><li>Ultrasound Bio-microscopy (UBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ophthalmic ultrasound imaging system market comprises several key types. A-Scan provides axial measurements of the eye, primarily for determining lens power. B-Scan offers two-dimensional cross-sectional images, aiding in the diagnosis of ocular conditions. Combined Scans integrate both A and B techniques for comprehensive analysis. Pachymeters specifically measure corneal thickness, crucial for glaucoma assessment. Ultrasound Biomicroscopy (UBM) delivers high-resolution images of anterior segment structures, enhancing diagnostic capabilities. Together, these modalities advance ocular diagnostics and treatment planning.</p></p>
