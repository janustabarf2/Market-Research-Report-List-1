<p><strong>The VOC Testing Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC testing device market is primarily divided into two types: PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID devices utilize ultraviolet light to ionize VOC molecules, providing rapid and sensitive detection across a wide range of chemicals, making them suitable for environmental monitoring and industrial safety. In contrast, MOS sensors rely on the conductivity change of metal oxide materials in response to the presence of VOCs, offering a cost-effective option for indoor air quality assessments and general environmental applications.</p></p>
