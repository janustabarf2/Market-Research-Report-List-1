<p><p>The duct axial fan market is primarily divided into AC axial fans and DC axial fans. In contrast, DC axial fans function on direct current, offering higher energy efficiency, compact size, and quieter operation, making them ideal for residential, electronic, and energy-sensitive applications. Both types play essential roles in optimizing air circulation across various settings.</p></p>
