<p><p>The thermal spray feeder market consists of two primary types: wire form and powder form. The wire form utilizes metal wires that are melted and sprayed onto a surface, providing precise control over thickness and material properties. In contrast, the powder form involves the use of powdered materials that are heated and sprayed, allowing for a broader range of material selections and coatings with varied textures. Both types are essential for applications in industries like aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing for enhancing surface characteristics.</p></p>
