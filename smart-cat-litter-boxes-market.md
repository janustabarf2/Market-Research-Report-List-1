<p><ul><li>Automatic Flush</li><li>Semi-Automatic Flush</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart cat litter box market can be divided into two main types: automatic flush and semi-automatic flush. Automatic flush litter boxes autonomously eliminate waste by using sensors to detect when a cat exits, flushing away waste without user intervention. In contrast, semi-automatic flush litter boxes require minimal user input, often needing the owner to trigger a flush or clean cycle after a set period. Both types aim to enhance convenience, hygiene, and reduce the frequency of manual cleaning.</p></p>
