<p><p>The disposable infusion bag market includes various types based on materials used: PVC, polypropylene, and polyethylene. PVC bags are widely utilized due to their flexibility and barrier properties. Polypropylene bags offer enhanced chemical resistance and lower leaching potential, making them suitable for sensitive medications. Polyethylene bags are known for their lightweight nature and cost-effectiveness but may provide less stability. Each material has distinct advantages, influencing healthcare providers' choices based on safety, stability, and compatibility with various infusion solutions.</p></p>
