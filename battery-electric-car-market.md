<p><p>The Battery Electric Car market primarily includes two main types: storage battery vehicles and fuel cell vehicles. Storage battery electric vehicles (BEVs) use rechargeable lithium-ion batteries to store energy and power electric motors, offering zero emissions and requiring charging from the grid. In contrast, fuel cell electric vehicles (FCEVs) generate electricity on-board through hydrogen fuel cells, emitting only water vapor. Both types contribute to sustainable transportation, with distinct advantages in energy sourcing and range capabilities, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
