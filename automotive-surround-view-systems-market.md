<p><p>Automotive surround-view systems enhance vehicle safety by providing a comprehensive view of the surroundings. They primarily come in three types: 4-camera systems use four strategically placed cameras to create a 360-degree view, ideal for parking and maneuvering. 6-camera systems add two more cameras, offering improved resolution and additional coverage, often incorporating features like bird’s-eye view or dynamic object tracking. Other systems may integrate advanced technologies like radar or LiDAR, enhancing situational awareness beyond traditional camera setups.</p></p>
